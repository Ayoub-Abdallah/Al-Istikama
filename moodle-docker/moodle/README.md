# Istikama Platform

A modern multi-school educational management and learning platform designed to connect administrators, school managers, teachers, students, and parents through a unified digital ecosystem.

## Overview

Istikama provides educational institutions with powerful tools for:

* Multi-school administration
* Student management
* Teacher management
* Academic lifecycle management
* Attendance tracking
* Homework and assignments
* Quizzes and assessments
* Digital library and content management
* Announcements and communication
* Analytics and reporting
* Parent engagement
* School performance monitoring

The platform is built to simplify educational operations while enhancing the learning experience for all stakeholders.

## Key Features

* 🏫 Multi-School Architecture
* 👨‍🏫 Teacher & Staff Management
* 👨‍🎓 Student Information System
* 📚 Learning & Assessment Tools
* 📖 Digital Library & Content Bank
* 📢 Announcements & Notifications
* 📊 Advanced Reports & Analytics
* 👪 Parent Follow-Up & Engagement
* 🌐 Multilingual Support (Arabic, French, English)
* 📱 Responsive Design Across All Devices

## Technology

Istikama is built on top of Moodle and extended with custom modules, workflows, dashboards, and user experiences tailored for modern educational institutions.
